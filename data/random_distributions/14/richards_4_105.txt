

PAMELA: or, Virtue Rewarded.
In a SERIES of Familiar Letters From a Beautiful Young Damsel to her Parents: And afterwards, In her EXALTED CONDITION, between Her, and Persons of Figure and Quality, upon the Most Important and Entertaining Subjects, In Genteel Life.
In Four Volumes.
Publish'd in order to cultivate the Principles of Virtue and Religion in the Minds of the YOUTH of BOTH SEXES.
The Third Edition, Corrected.
