

* The historical part of the American Register, written by Mr. Brown,
would make an 8vo. of about 400 pages. And the republication of it
might be useful.
* This able work was entirely completed at his decease, except the part
relating to the United States. It has never yet been published. A gentleman,
who was a native of Britain, and perfectly acquainted with the
subject, and who had read the manuscript of the account of London,
contained in this work, declared it to be, beyond comparison, the best
history of that city which he had ever seen.
