CHAP. II.
Mr. Booth continues his Story. In this
Chapter there are some Passages that may
serve as a Kind of Touchstone, by which
a young Lady may examine the Heart of
her Lover. I would advise, therefore,
that every Lover be obliged to read it over
in the Presence of his Mistress, and that
she carefully watch his Emotions while he
is reading. p. 102
